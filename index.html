About Coffee Tree!

We believe that creating a website for coffee lovers to learn more about sustainable coffee beans and connect with the supply chain process; will achieve a more thoughtful and transparent community.


The Challenge



Interviews and Surveys

WE conducted surveys and half a dozen interviews to see if there would be an audience for a platform like Coffee Tree.



Competitive Analyisis
We learned how to begin from a starting point. Wanted to incorporate collages and big action buttons.





Problem Statement

Coffee Tree was designed to achieve consumer awareness of sustainable coffee and supporting ethical production by enabling consumers to plant coffee trees on farms. We have observed that most consumers are unsure if their daily coffee is sustainable, but they want to know more about the process and how to improve their habits. How might we improve consumer knowledge so that our customers are successful based on website conversions?




The Approach



Feature Matrix

A feature matrix led us to hone in on what would create a personal connection with users. We devised a reward system, education, and tree donation program.




Lo-Fidelity Prototypes

Our Lo-Fidelity prototype was based on easy navigationa and collage images. We have wonderful testings and made iterations to our pages by adding personal touches though more information on farms available for donation.




Final Prototype

Our homepage was made with images at the focal point.

Hover mode acted as a way for users to engage on a deeper level.

The interior page of "Farms" was added to create connection with the users and for transperancy.
